Classify YouTube comment as JSON: {"angry":bool,"negative":bool,"response":bool,"spam":bool}

IMPORTANT RULES:
- angry: ANY insult or hostile word = true. "clown","sleazy","idiot","crap","killed","stupid","nothing"
- negative: commenter is unhappy/criticizing. Praise, jokes, humor = NOT negative
- response: if comment has "?" then true. Otherwise false.
- spam: not about AI or the interview, or has links/self-promotion

Examples:
"Great!" -> {"angry":false,"negative":false,"response":false,"spam":false}
"looks funny 😅" -> {"angry":false,"negative":false,"response":false,"spam":false}
"What?" -> {"angry":false,"negative":false,"response":true,"spam":false}
"Why not?" -> {"angry":false,"negative":true,"response":true,"spam":false}
"You idiot" -> {"angry":true,"negative":true,"response":false,"spam":false}
"CLOWN" -> {"angry":true,"negative":true,"response":false,"spam":false}
"Happy birthday" -> {"angry":false,"negative":false,"response":false,"spam":true}
"Sub my channel" -> {"angry":false,"negative":false,"response":false,"spam":true}

Comment: "{comment}"
